FAQs

1. What are your restaurant's opening hours?

We are open from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM from Monday to Sunday.

2. Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes on our menu. Check out the "Healthy Choices" section.

3. Can I make a reservation?

Yes, you can make reservations by calling us at (123) 456-7890 or through our online reservation system.

4. Do you provide home delivery services?

Yes, we provide home delivery services within a 10-mile radius. You can place your order online or via our app.

5. Do you accommodate food allergies?

Absolutely. Please inform us of any food allergies when placing your order, and we will ensure your meal is prepared safely.

6. Do you have parking facilities?

Yes, we have ample parking space for our customers, including accessible parking spots.

7. Do you host private events or parties?

Yes, we host private events and parties. Contact us to discuss your requirements and customize your event package.
